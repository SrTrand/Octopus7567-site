    Team Areas

    The team works with divisions structure, each one have subareas and do the work that was designed. They are:

    Automation

    The automation subareas is Mechanical and Eletrics. They are responsable for build the intakes, the drivetrain and the electral parts of the robot.

    Programming

    Programming is responsable for the program of the robot, using LabView language, giving life to this machine.

    Marketing

    The marketing subareas is Adminstration, Public Relationships, Scouting e Design. They write all the documents, make contact with other teams and community and the visual identity of the team.

    #7567

    The SESI/SENAI Octopus #7567 team was created in August 2018 to participate in the First Robotics Competition (FRC). It is composed to students of articulated teaching SESI / SENAI who work in the knowledge areas of automation (mechanical and electrical), programming, design and marketing.
