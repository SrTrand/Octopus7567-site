History

The FRC team SESI/SENAI Octopus #7567 on it actual formation was
founded in august of 2018, but the students and mentors have an
vast experience with others robotics championships like FIRST Lego
League (FLL), World Robotics Olympiad (WRO) and Olimpíada Brasi-
leira de Robótica (OBR). The both schools involved in the project do
a lot of investments in programs that involve students on robotics,
science, technology and were interested for a long time in envolve
teenagers on FRC.

Once the team has been defined, we research and learn everything
it was possible about the FRC championship, FIRST concepts and the
challenges which we would have to overcome. The harmony was the
best possible, soon we were all connected by the same line of reason-
ing, knowing everything we should do to exceed our limits. Even divid-
ed into areas such as Marketing, Automation, Programming and
Design, we all have the same great goal of bringing our knowledge
and the FIRST message to throughout the community. Today the
team is made up of 11 members (students) and, as an octopus, we
have several tentacles, but together we form only one and powerful
mind, ready to overcome challenges and solve the problems that can
hinder us in this journey, that's why we chose that name.

Volunteer Work

There is no doubt that one of the most rewarding stages of the preseason
is the contact with the community. The exchange and knowledge builds
little by little the history of our team.

During the 2018/2019 preseason our team did workshops, presentations,
participated in events and visits public schools who don't have robotics teaching.

Octopus Values

This are the values that we always respect and honor.

Clearly Organization:

We work with an clearly and efficient plan of action.

Respect our differences:

Every person is different from others, we're unique and amazing exactly the way we are. Everyone have a thing they do better, so we respect these aptitudes and organize it to do the right thing.

Fun and Appreciation:

We have fun, love and appreciate the tasks that we develop, making the journey easier and lighter.

Determination and Unity

We are focused and strong on the purpose, we don't give up, never let down and have faith in ourselves. We push up each others, together, never get separated and always think as a team.

#7567

The SESI/SENAI Octopus #7567 team was created in August 2018 to participate in the First Robotics Competition (FRC). It is composed to students of articulated teaching SESI / SENAI who work in the knowledge areas of automation (mechanical and electrical), programming, design and marketing.
